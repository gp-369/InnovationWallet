What is Online Banking?

Online banking allows you to manage your finances through a bank's website or mobile app. It offers convenience and accessibility for everyday banking needs.

Benefits of Online Banking

  • 24/7 access to your accounts.
  • Ability to pay bills and transfer funds instantly.
  • Monitor transactions and account balances in real time.
  • Set up alerts for low balances or suspicious activity.

Online Banking Security Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords for your accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication for added security.
  • Avoid logging into your account on public Wi-Fi.
  • Monitor your accounts regularly for unauthorized transactions.
