Introduction to Loans

Loans are agreements between lenders and borrowers where a sum of money is borrowed with the promise of repayment, often with interest, over a specified period.

Common Types of Loans

  • Personal Loans: Unsecured loans used for various purposes like consolidating debt or covering unexpected expenses.
  • Auto Loans: Secured loans for purchasing a vehicle, where the car serves as collateral.
  • Home Loans: Long-term loans for buying a home, commonly referred to as mortgages.
  • Student Loans: Loans designed to help students pay for education expenses, often with lower interest rates.
  • Payday Loans: Short-term, high-interest loans meant to cover immediate cash needs until the next paycheck.
  • Small Business Loans: Loans designed to support startups or growing businesses.

Loan Management Tips

  • Understand the loan terms before signing.
  • Make payments on time to avoid penalties.
  • Borrow only what you need and can repay comfortably.
  • Consider the total cost of the loan, including interest rates and fees.
  • Monitor your credit to improve loan eligibility.
