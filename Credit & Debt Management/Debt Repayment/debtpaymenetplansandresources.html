Introduction to Debt Repayment

Debt repayment plans are structured strategies to pay off your debts efficiently. Understanding the options available can help reduce financial stress and improve financial stability.

Debt Repayment Plans

  • Snowball Method: Pay off smaller debts first, then move to larger ones, gaining momentum as you go.
  • Avalanche Method: Focus on paying debts with the highest interest rates first to minimize total interest paid.
  • Debt Consolidation: Combine multiple debts into a single loan with a lower interest rate.
  • Debt Management Plans: Work with credit counseling agencies to create a feasible repayment plan.

Debt Repayment Resources

  • Credit counseling agencies like NFCC (National Foundation for Credit Counseling).
  • Debt repayment calculators to estimate timelines and costs.
  • Government resources for student loan repayment programs.
  • Support groups and online forums for financial advice.
