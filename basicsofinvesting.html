What is Investing?

Investing involves putting money into assets like stocks, bonds, or real estate with the goal of growing your wealth over time.

Why Start Investing?

  • Grow your wealth through compound interest.
  • Achieve financial goals like retirement or education.
  • Protect your money from inflation.

Basic Steps to Start

  • Understand your financial goals and risk tolerance.
  • Start small and invest consistently.
  • Diversify your investments to minimize risks.
