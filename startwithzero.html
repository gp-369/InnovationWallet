Start with Zero

Can You Start Investing with Zero?

Yes, many investment platforms today allow you to start with minimal or no initial capital, making it easier to begin your journey.

Ways to Start

  • Use micro-investing apps like Acorns or Stash.
  • Sign up for robo-advisors with no minimum investment.
  • Participate in employer-sponsored retirement plans like 401(k)s.

Why Start Small?

Starting small builds confidence, creates habits, and allows you to learn without risking large amounts of money.
