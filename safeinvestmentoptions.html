What are Safe Investments?

Safe investments are low-risk financial products that help preserve your money while offering modest returns.

Examples of Safe Investments

  • High-yield savings accounts
  • Certificates of deposit (CDs)
  • Government bonds (e.g., U.S. Treasury bonds)
  • Money market accounts

Benefits of Safe Investments

They provide stability, liquidity, and protection of principal, making them ideal for conservative investors.
