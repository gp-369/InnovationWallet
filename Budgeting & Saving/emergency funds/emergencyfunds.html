What is an Emergency Fund?

An emergency fund is a financial safety net for unexpected expenses like medical bills, car repairs, or sudden loss of income.

Why Have an Emergency Fund?

  • Provides financial security during unforeseen circumstances.
  • Helps avoid debt from high-interest credit cards or loans.
  • Reduces stress during emergencies.

  • Automate your savings to stay consistent.
  • Cut unnecessary expenses and redirect that money to your fund.
