Steps to Create a Simple Budget

  • Calculate your total monthly income after taxes.
  • List all your monthly expenses, such as rent, utilities, groceries, and entertainment.
  • Categorize expenses into fixed (e.g., rent) and variable (e.g., dining out).
  • Allocate a portion of your income for savings and emergencies.
  • Track your spending and adjust your budget as needed.

Budgeting Tips

Use the 50/30/20 rule: 50% of income for needs, 30% for wants, and 20% for savings or debt repayment.
